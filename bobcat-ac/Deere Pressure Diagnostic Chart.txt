Diagnostic Chart for Air Conditioning System
The diagnostic chart can help to understand how different conditions can affect the entire system. The chart gives symptoms that normally are associated with the conditions that are listed.
Use the Troubleshooting Charts and the Flow Charts for Diagnosing the System to locate the source of a problem and systematically examine and repair the problem.
