Essential AC Service Tools
The tools and equipment required for safe and effective Bobcat AC system service.
Manifold Gauge Set

High-quality manifold gauges are essential for reading system pressures and performing recovery/recharge operations.

  • Red hose: High pressure side
  • Blue hose: Low pressure side
  • Yellow hose: Recovery/recharge
  • Pressure ranges: 0-500 PSI high, 0-150 PSI low
Recovery Machine

Refrigerant recovery is required by EPA regulations. The Mastercool 69110 is commonly used for Bobcat service.

  • Recovers refrigerant without venting
  • Separates oil from refrigerant
  • Meets EPA 608 certification requirements
  • Required for all AC service work
Vacuum Pump

Used to evacuate air and moisture from the AC system before charging with refrigerant.

  • Creates deep vacuum (500 microns or less)
  • Removes moisture from system
  • Essential for proper system operation
  • Typical evacuation time: 30-60 minutes
Electronic Scale

Precision scale for measuring refrigerant quantity during recovery and charging operations.

  • Accurate to 0.1 oz
  • Required for proper charging
  • Tracks recovered refrigerant amount
  • Essential for documentation
Leak Detection Tools

Various methods for detecting refrigerant leaks in Bobcat AC systems.

  • Electronic leak detectors (UV or heated diode)
  • UV dye and black light
  • Soap bubble solution
  • Nitrogen pressure testing
Temperature Tools

Infrared and contact probes for measuring temperatures during AC system operation.

  • Infrared non-contact works well for duct temps
  • DVOM temperature probes work well for condenser and evaporator temps

Equipment Maintenance
Proper care and maintenance of AC service tools.
Tool Care Tips

Keep all tools clean and calibrated. Replace hoses that show signs of wear or damage. Store equipment in clean, dry conditions to prevent contamination.
