Exercise 1: Verify Proper Operation

Test conditions: Install a gauge set and lower the cab if necessary. Start the machine and operate af half throttle. AC on full. Give it 5 minutes.

  • Document temps and feel the amount of air flow at the ducts. Compare upper to lower ducts.
  • Fill in your observations table
  • Compare readings to 8 times rule of thumb
Equipment needed: Manifold gauges, Thermometer, Stopwatch
Exercise 2: Refrigerant Charge

Test Conditions: Recycling machine installed. Adjust refrigerant charge as described below then operate the machine at half throttle, AC on full.

  • Recover the refrigerant and recharge with only 0.5lb. Operate machine and fill in your observations table
  • Does your machine have a refrigerant sight glass? Any bubbles?
  • Recover the refrigerant and recharge with 3lb. Operate machine and fill in your observations table
Equipment needed: AC recycling machine, Thermometer, Stopwatch
Exercise 3: Airflow Testing

Test Conditions: Gauge set installed. Operate the machine at half throttle, AC on full.

  • Block the Condencer with cardboard. Fill in your Observations Table
  • Move blockage to cab recirc filter - Fill in your Observations Table
  • Record your observations of the suction line. Is it cold, wet, or frozen?
Equipment needed: Thermometer, Bobcat machine, clean filters
Exercise 4: Electrical Diagnostics

Practice electrical troubleshooting on Bobcat AC systems.

  • Bypass compressor clutch using high-current test light
  • Bypass pressure switch function (cuts out below 28 psi / above 384 psi on high side)
  • Voltage drop power circuit to MCC
Equipment needed: Multimeter, test leads, High-current test light, wiring diagrams
Exercise 5: Heater Valve Leaking

A common failure. The AC is working correctly, however, the heater core is hot and the AC cannot overcome it. Turn the HVAC temperature control switch to medium. Install a gauge set. Start the machine and operate af half throttle. AC on full. Give it 5 minutes.

  • Measure the duct temperature and notice the air flow amount.
  • Install a gauge set. Note the compressor cycle times.
  • Feel the heater core inlet and outlet lines.
Equipment needed: Gauge set, Thermometer,
Exercise 6: Identify stuck TXV

Test conditions: Install gausge set, operate machine at half-throttle with AC on full.

  • Use a heat gun to warm the TXV. Fill in your observation table.
  • Use an ice cube to cool the TXV. Fill in your observation table.
Equipment needed: Gauge set, Heat gun, Ice cube.
